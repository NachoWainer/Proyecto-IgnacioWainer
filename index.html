About Us

We serve 100% plant based food which fusions a variety of flavors from all over the world. Our little venture traces back to 2020 when the covid-19 pandemic forced us to lockdown. With that gloomy scenario, we decided to take advantage of time and started experimenting with ingredients and new flavors. Almost two years later, we have come up with an innovative proposal, which we decided was time to introduce it to this new world. We hope to see you soon!
